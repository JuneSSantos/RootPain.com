WAD Information & Description

File Name: rootpain.wad
Release Date: July 8, 2013
Genre: Horror
WAD Type: Total Conversion
WAD Length: Short
WAD Difficulty: Easy
Working Sourceports: ZDoom (GZDoom breaks scripting)

Description:
RootPain.WAD, this is it, this is the real deal. Hell, I've even named
the website around this WAD, and despite how simple and short it is
I legitimately love this WAD with a burning passion

You play as a parasite inside a womb-like world, and your objective
is to destroy the two ovaries on the map, once you're done destroying
them, you'll get a special surprise. But how are you going to be able
to destroy them without any weapons? It's up to you to find out.

I mostly adore the atmosphere of this WAD, there's something both
unsettling and comfortable about this whole thing that I legitimately
can't put my finger into, and the fact this WAD received such a
visceral reaction, even though it was mostly made for a quick laugh
makes me love it even further, although I am dissapointed he never
made an extended version of this WAD like he said he would back in
the day, although RootPain did return in another form as he made
a reference and tribute to the WAD in one of his other DooM projects
called "Solace Dreams" where he essentially remakes the RootPain WAD
for one of the levels in the game.

Give RootPain.WAD a try, I'm sure you'll end up loving it.
