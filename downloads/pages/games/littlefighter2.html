Game Information & Description

Game Name: Little Fighter 2
Release Date: 1999
Creators: Marti Wong & Starsky Wong
Genre: Beat Em' Up/Fighter
Working Systems: Windows XP, Vista, 7, 10, 11, Linux (Via Wine/Proton)

Description: Little Fighter 2 is a Beat Em' Up/Fighting Game in which
you can take control of a colorful cast of characters, all of them
with their own unique strenghts and gimmicks, and with a variety
of modes for you to enjoy yourself in, from arcade, versus, horde mode
tournament mode, and even tag team tournaments

I used to play this game a ton when I was a kid, and I used to edit
a ton of stages and put a ton of characters into my game aswell
because this game can be easily modded if you take your time
to learn it. meaning you can have custom characters, custom songs
custom stages, or even just outright completely overhaul and make an
entirely new game out of your modifications

But even without modifying your game, this game already provides a ton
of fun out of the box, the gameplay, although weird in a few aspects
is a tad fun once you get the hang of it, specially once you figure out
how to pull special moves, and the basics of the combat
There's a ton of characters to play around, all of them playing
completely differently from each other, from ranged characters
to mages, and even some samurais and archers, and all of these
characters stand out from each other, if not in appearance
in their movesets and gameplay. some of these characters even
inspired some of my characters (they're< pretty obvious once
you compare them), and this game continues to be one of the best
games you could play out there, it stood the test of time, and the
creator recently told he's remaking the game from the ground up
but even then, this game is still really sweet, from having a simple
yet interesting gameplay, with a ton of secrets and cool little details
you'll find while playing the game, to the fact you can modify the game
to fit your tastes, you'll end up loving Little Fighter 2 just as much
as I did if you give it the chance.

Play Little Fighter 2 yourself, it's good, it's free, it runs on
anything, and it's just plain fun, you won't regret it.
