RootPain's Commentary

Look how amazing he looks...
Isn't he pretty?
Doesn't he have the nicest smile you've ever seen?
But through these beady eyes, you can see that he
carries a lot of hurt
But you know what they say; "No crying until the end."

This is still one of the best things anyone has ever made for me
Beatum is so versatile with his art it's genuinely scary
One night he'll make a goofy art with incredibly expressive emotions
And in the next night, he'll draw a hyper-detailed painting of you with details
and accuracy that would challenge Leonardo Da Vinci and Picasso head on

I always loved how he understood that my character is canonically supposed
to be very sad and melancholic, with most of the art he made for me being
mostly him being very neutral or just slightly happy, with the little times
he's genuinely happy being when he's near his family and Caira
These fanarts are still genuinely one of the best things I've ever seen
I love the detail, the sketchy and messy lines, his sad look
his subtle smile and beady eyes, it's perfect

But hey, it's Beatum
I don't expect nothing but the best coming from him.

But that's it for now
Keep on trying, keep on ridin'
We'll meet again.
