June's Commentary

More fanart!!!
Can you guess from who it is?
Beatum pretty much owns this section at this point
It's a surprise I didn't name the whole fanart section
after him considering most of my fanart folder consists
mostly of him and his art, but either way, it's always
nice to see more out of him, so I'm not complaining.

What can I say about this without having to repeat myself considering every
every single FUCKING time I talk about Beatum's art it's just me sucking his
dick harder than a vacuum ever wish it could suck?

I can't, and to be honest, it's deserved

In a lot of ways, Beatum gets my character better than I do, that might be
because he's just that good of an artists, but a lot of the design choices
he made with this little guy that's supposed to be my biggest creation and
the way he portrays him always leave me satisified

Big Whiskey was always meant to be a sad, or at least a melancholic
character, his whole story and design was created around this fact
A deceptively very friendly looking character that just so happens
to be really troubled and in a lot of ways incredible angsty about everyone
and everything, and Beatum always drew him like he's tired of everything
And I fucking love it.

And this piece perfectly captures why
It's not anything dramatic, it's just him sitting there, visibly very
tired, something's bothering him, but you don't know what
It's very subtle and I love this kind of shit

This piece is still one of my absolute favorites, and I have nothing
but love towards it, in general, everything that Beatum does puts a smile
in my face, so seeing either new or old stuff from him always puts me
in a good mood :)

But anyways, that's about it
I'll see you soon once I get to update this thing again, bye!

Big Sad
