RootPain's Commentary

Oohhhh, more fanart from Beatum!
Let's make this quick, I've been working on this update
the whole day, let's get this over with so I can go home
and go to sleep.

This one is quite cool in particular, because Beatum decided to give
my little character a different jacket design, with a semi-futuristic
vibe to it, and a blue color scheme, and surprisingly
It fits quite well!

I'm normally against adding too many colors to my designs since I feel
like sticking with around three colors usually makes the design pop far
more than slamming my characters with tons of details, but the blue
contrasts so well with the yellows, and the design of the jacket itself
is so slick, I can't help but love it

There's also some other drawings of Big Whiskeys in here too
One where he actually brushed his hair... and yes, Big Whiskey's
messy hair isn't his natural hair, it's just that he forgets to brush
his hair regularly, and we can actually see how it's supposed to look
when brushed here!

And then we have Big Whiskey with a short hair
And fun fact; he's based on how Beatum drew his own hair
and I like this idea a lot too, he looks real nice with short hair

As usual, all of these are very cool!

But anyways, that's it for today
See ya soon!... eventually.
