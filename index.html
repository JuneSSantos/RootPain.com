Quick Update

It's been a while, I know.
I've said this a bunch of times already, I know.
So I'll keep it brief and simple, since this ain't a happy one.

I've been thinking about things a ton recently, and I've been planning to
remove a bunch of things from it, since I can't even look at my own
website without feeling a lot of mixed feelings, all which range from
sad, to angry, and even betrayed and dissapointed

But giving up on it doesn't feel right, I've poured my soul and heart
into every little detail and page of this website, and even my own money
is going down the drain to keep this website alive
And deep down, I still feel a ton of love for it, and I still want to try
make everything work out, but it's becoming increasingly tiring to have
to put up a losing fight at every step, despite the best of my intentions

So when I can't improve the situation, I might aswell cut my losses
and try and move on with whatever I still have left

I can take all the hits in the world, and I can put up a fight every
day of my life, but I don't want to, I legitimately just want to enjoy
what's little to enjoy of this miserable life until the gates of hell
welcome me back once again.

So yeah, it's very likely that you're going to see a lot of things
changed or gone from here in the next time you see any significant updates

I know I promised the Blog Section and other things, but I'm trying
my hardest to settle on designs, how to approach everything, but nothing
seems to work, and I don't ever want to rush things out of the door
just for the sake of having it up, so no, the plans aren't gone
I'm legitimately just lost.

I've tried my best
Maybe one day it will be enough, but I think it's going to be too
too late until then, but at least I can say that I've gone down fighting
I don't give up, but there's only so much a person can handle

Either way, time is running out
Keep the dream alive, or chase a better one
The struggle continues.

I'll see you when I see you.
