what is rootpain.com?

RootPain.com is all things considered my passion project that I've been building up
for years now, but only now it's fully realised

RootPain.com, formerly known as JuneSSai.net is a tribute to the era of the internet
that I grew up in, mostly inspired on The Mystical Forest Zone and countless other
sources like Power Sonic, Something Awful, Newgrounds and etc.
I made with the intent of recreating and succeeding on what those sites were trying
to do back in the day, to make something special in which people would come back
from time to time to see what's new, or simply because they just enjoyed the site
that much.

RootPain.com was also made out of spite, to prove that Modern Web Design is absolute
dogshit, and not because of a lack of technology, development tools or resources
but because everyone is opting to be lazy and making their sites dull and boring
while simultaneously making them hard to navigate for no reason

One of the main objectives with RootPain.com was to make a site that's not only
interesting to look at at every page, but you'd be able to find everything you
want or need easily without having to compromise visual detail or style
And the second objective making something memorable, something that someone maybe
in a few years in the future will remember fondly, and they'll say to themselves
"I want to see that again, and maybe one day, make something like that too"
just like how those sites did for me back in the day, they inspired me, and I hope
that I can inspire someone else out there aswell.
